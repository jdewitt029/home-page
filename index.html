Jordan DeWitt


It is not wise to judge others based on your own preconceptions and by their appearances.

I am an aspiring artist that wants to leave a postive impression on anyone who views my art. Digital art making through various programs and software is something I have learned thanks to the Digital Media Art program at San Jose State University. I was introduced to different technology that would allow me to create artworks using various mediums and techniques. I am a relatively positive person and use art to try and uplift others with the content of my work. The soul and spirituality is a constant theme in my work and is something I will focus on throughout my art career. If I am able to change the life of one person through art, then I have accomplished my goal as an artist.
