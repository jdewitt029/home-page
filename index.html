It is not wise to judge others based on your own preconceptions and by their appearances.

Digital art through various programs and software is something I have learned thanks to the Digital Media Art program at San Jose State University. I was introduced to different technology that would allow me to create artworks using various mediums and techniques. I have always enjoyed creating graphics with lots of visual effects, which will be the majority of my work to this point. However, I have been introduced to game development and this is a field that I would love to go into if an opportunity is available. Creative expression through art, games, graphics, or anything else is something that I am looking forward to doing as a career moving forward.
