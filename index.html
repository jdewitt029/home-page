Jordan DeWitt


It is not wise to judge others based on your own preconceptions and by their appearances.

I am currently a student at San Jose State University enrolled in the Digital Media art (DMA) program. Before coming to SJSU, I was mostly interested in creating Photoshop works that were either posters or cover art for music. After a few semesters in this program, I have been exposed to many different programs and software that have broadened my horizons. I hope to keep learning new things that interest me and I am excited to see where my future takes me. My portolio consists of projects using the new programs and software I have learned along with some of my earlier works that I made before transferring to SJSU.
